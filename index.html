About Me

Bluring the lines of digital and physical

"A jack of all trades, master of non, thou are better than the master one" – Adam Savage. This is how I would describe myself. From a young age, I’ve loved movies and video games, so I decided to study Computer Graphics and Media Design at Victoria University to experience the best of both worlds.

I’m a passionate maker and technical artist specializing in 3D, programming, and costume and prop design. My enthusiasm for this work has allowed me to develop a diverse skill set that bridges the gap between creative expression and technical execution.

Current Occupation: Junior Lighting Technical Director @ Weta FX
