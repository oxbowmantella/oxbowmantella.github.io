About Me

Bluring the lines of digital and physical

"A Jack of all trades, master on non, thou are better than the master one" - Adam Savage. This is how I would describe myself and a young age I've loved movies and video games, so I decided to study Computer Graphics and Media Design, at Victoria University, so I could experience the best of both worlds.

So now I am a passionate maker, and technical artist, specialising in 3D, programming, along with costume and prop design/manufacturing. My passion for this work has enabled me to develop a broad set of skills that bridge the gap between the creative and technical worlds.

Current Occupation: Assistant Technical Director @ Weta FX
